Frequently Asked Questions

What is Algo Trading?

To place a trade, an algorithmic trading system uses a computer software that follows a set of instructions. In theory, the trade can make profits at a pace and frequency that would be hard for a human trader to achieve.

What is a trading bot?

A trading algo, often known as a robot, is a computer programme that can identify buy and sell opportunities as well as execute entry and exit orders. To be profitable, the robot must be able to spot market inefficiencies on a regular and consistent basis.
